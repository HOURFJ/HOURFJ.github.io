Eazy Lasagna

An eazy lasagna recipe that saves much time. Will statisfy everyone in the family! Try it out!

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Cottage cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Dried ground parsley
  • lasagna noodles
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Cook and drain ground beef, stir in spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine cheeses, egg, and seasonings into a large bowl.
  3. Use 3/4 cups sauce in 9X13 baking dish cover with 3 noodles.
  4. Repeat with cheese mix.
  5. Repeat steps 3-4 twice.
  6. Bake @ 350 Â°F for 45 min.
  7. Enjoy.
