Buttery Garlic Green Beans

Use fresh green beans for the best taste. Eazy and flavorful! Pair with the "Eazy Lasagna" recipe!

Ingredients:

  • Fresh green beans (trimmed and snapped)
  • Butter
  • Garlic, minced
  • lemon pepper seasoning
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Place green beans into large skillet, cover with water. Boil. reduce and simmer for 3-5 min.
  2. Drain and return to skillet. Add butter until melted.
  3. Add butter and cook until fragrent.
  4. Season with lemon pepper seasoing.
