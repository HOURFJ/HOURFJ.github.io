Fruit Punch (Non Alcoholic)

Eazy fruit punch! Pair with naughty children and have a calm evening.

Ingredients:

  • Ginger ale, chilled
  • Fruit punch, chilled
  • Unsweetened Pineapple Juice, chilled
  • Orange sherbet

Steps:

  1. Combine all into a large bowl or drink dispenser.
  2. Once all the Sherbet melts mix and serve.
